Meet Raspberry Pi®

The Raspberry Pi is a credit-card sized computer that plugs into your TV and a keyboard. It’s a capable little PC which can be used for many of the things that your desktop PC does, like spreadsheets, word-processing and games. It also plays high-definition video. We want to see it being used by kids all over the world to learn programming. We plan to use Raspberry Pi as the base of our hardware, along with the tweaked version of the camera module to enable night vision, in order to make it the ultimate surveillance system for your home!

After analyzing frames through motion detection algorithms, your photos will be automatically pushed to your wifi network and from there to your mobile device, so you will have instant notification if someone's right outside your door, wherever you are!

For the wireless communication we will be using Miniature WiFi Module which will enchance Raspberry Pi with WiFi capabilities!
